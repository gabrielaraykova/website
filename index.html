About the Designer

With a background in fine arts and textile design, Ivanka Raykova brings a unique perspective to contemporary fashion that honors traditional craftsmanship. Inspired by natural landscapes and cultural heritage, each piece is thoughtfully crafted to blend modern silhouettes with traditional embroidery techniques.

The IVANKA RAYKOVA label celebrates the beauty of traditional embroidery and craftsmanship while creating elegant, minimalist designs in a predominantly white and neutral color palette that allows the intricate detailing to take center stage.

Each collection reflects a commitment to preserving cultural heritage, supporting local artisans, and creating timeless pieces that connect the past with the present—designs that tell a story while embracing modern aesthetics.

The Atelier

Located in a bright, airy space that reflects our aesthetic, our atelier serves as both design studio and showroom. Here, traditional embroidery techniques meet contemporary design, with each piece carefully crafted to honor cultural heritage.

We work closely with master embroiderers and artisans who specialize in traditional techniques, preserving these crafts while reimagining them for modern sensibilities. Every embroidery pattern tells a story and carries symbolic meaning from our cultural traditions.

Our commitment to craftsmanship extends to every aspect of production, from sourcing natural, sustainable fabrics to the final hand-finished details. We welcome clients by appointment for custom consultations and fittings, offering a personalized experience that connects them to the stories behind our designs.
